
A Kingdom set on a peninsula, with a large wall separating the city from the rest of the land, and water surrounding the other sides. A large watching eye looms over the city as a form of surveillance and protection, for both outside and inside.

## Climate

Warm, Mediterranean climate.

- Hot dry summers
    
- Wet, mild winters
    

## Points of Interest / Map (Aaron + Saavan)

### Rutmar Port

- Main large port in the city, located on the east
    
- Rests in a gulf that occurs within the larger continental mass
    
- Merchant ships and other commercial vessels dock here
    
- Large amount of guards and strict surveillance to keep track on money dealings
    

  

### Mansum Village

- Village that lies right beside the port, facing the seaside
    
- Used to be primarily a small fishing village, but once Rutmar Port was built, the fishermen were driven away
    
- Now, it primarily consists of lodge owners who make revenue by housing merchants and outlanders
    
- The guards took the town under government control to monitor outlanders and hired the lodge owners as “sellouts”, in a sense
    
- Only select few outlanders are allowed to stay beyond the town boundary and must be accompanied by a guard at all times

### The Broken Ravine

- Refers to the entire left coastline of the city
    
- The coastline consists of sharp, jagged cliffs and barbed wire fences to prevent people from trying to escape via seafare here (as well as prevent people from being smuggled into the city via this route)
