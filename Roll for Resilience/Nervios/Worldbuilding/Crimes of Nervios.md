A criminal standing accused of a crime faces a punishment that fits the severity of the crime. 

| Severity of Crime | Crimes                                                                                                                      | Potential Punishments                                                     |
| ----------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Infraction        | Loitering, vagrancy, breaking Common Decency Laws.                                                                          | Fines, Corporal Punishment                                                |
| Misdemeanor       | Petty theft, assault, trespassing, vandalism, failure to report a crime, disorderly conduct                                 | Community service, short jail sentence, probation, increased surveillance |
| High-Crime        | Sedition, treason, heresy, murder, attempted murder, grand larceny, market fraud, smuggling, illegal emigration/immigration | Prison, capital punishment                                                |
