*[[Nervios]] is the city that you know. You grew up here and you're familiar with the comings and goings of the city. Historically closed off due to wars and economic turmoil, the city of Nervios is isolated; with its impenetrable walls cutting it off from the rest of the continent. The peninsula has most of what the city needs; protection, food, water, stability. All this under the shadow of the [[The Nervios Eye|Nervios Eye]], the great central tower rooted in the bay that acts as a guardian of the city. Watching every move in the city. You know the rules of the city by heart now; keep your eyes down, speak only in pleasantries, and guard your every thought. A small trade for the protection of the city guard and the [[The Nervios Eye]].*

### [[Rebels]]:
Not many people are pleased with the direction the city has gone in, and some have organized into a silent rebellion against the guards. These rebels help maintain the burrow through smuggling in goods and maintaining the underground, while also on the look out for possible ways to decrease the influence of the guards and bring freedom and peace of mind back to the city of Nervios.
### Nobles:
[[Nervios]] was once a monarchy, but when the guards seized control, the various noble houses decided to help overthrow the king to gain more power in the aftermath. Currently, nobles use their vast wealth and influence to run most of the business in Nervios and get family members in positions of high ranking power in the guards to maintain their power in the city. Maybe noble families live in nice, one story buildings that afford them plenty of privacy and room, with the wealthiest family having an entire island to themselves.
### [[Church of Aequorism]]:
When the guards took over the city, some members of Umorism schemed to form the religion of Aequorism, which teaches reverence and attempts to placate the ocean instead of rising to its challenges. This religion found support among the guards as it taught obedience and conformity as the correct way to handle problems, which helped the guards tremendously. Currently, the church is a major power that holds massive sway over the ports as their blessings are seen as one of the only ways to safely cross the sea. 
### [[Sea Scribes of Umorism]]:
Nervios used to practice Umorism, which teaches that the sea is a greater challenger that spurs humanity forward into new innovations and discoveries, primarily through the development of new ship building techniques and technologies. When the guards took over, they outlawed Umorism as it was seen as encouraging leaving the walls of the city and promoting dubious thoughts. Now Umorism is practiced in secret, with many of the priests of Umorism, known as Sea Scribes, mainly teaching [[Rebels|Rebel]] groups ship building techniques to better smuggle in vital goods or attempting to find old ship building lore.
