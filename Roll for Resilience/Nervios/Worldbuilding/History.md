
- After a period of turmoil, a group of elite wizards/militants rise to power and enforce the spire in the middle of town to help maintain fear and control.
    

- Written in the perspective of how the citizens understand how the tower came into prominence.
    
- There was once a time before the watch, before the tower, before the undisturbed sight. It was a chaotic time, full of strife, pain, poverty, and criminals running amok. The world outside was rapidly closing in and fear was rampant throughout the city. From those ashes came the watchmen, guards banning together to reestablish order through magics that allowed them to keep constant vigilance of the city, and soon the tower rose. A monolith of the powers working to watch and protect the city from itself. It has stood here for generations, standing watch over the city and guiding the quick hand of the watch. To this day, the city only survives because of the watchmen, from the threats abroad and within. 
    
- The tower was raised roughly 150 years ago, which means that everyone that grew up in the city that isn't an elf or another long lived race lived with it always being present
    
- Notably the economic state of the city hasn’t changed that much actually besides the need for a black market to arise as the increased surveillance has made many products hard to get.
    
- Lately, counter surveillance sentiments have started to grow as the newer generations having to grow up under this tyrannical rule have started to rebel as they hunger for a life not driven by fear.
    

- They help propagate underground meeting places where people can mingle without fear of the eyes of the guards turning on them.
