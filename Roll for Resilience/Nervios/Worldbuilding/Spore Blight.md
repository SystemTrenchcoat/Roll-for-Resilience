
Upon inhaling the spores that carry this disease, the player must make a DC 13 Con save. On a failure, they are infected with the disease and suffer from the poisoned condition for 1d8 hours. After this incubation period, their constitution score decreases by 1 until it reaches 0. At a score of 12 or lower, those who come into close contact with them can be infected by inhaling their infected air. At a score of 8 or lower, they suffer a -1d4 penalty on charisma saves as mushrooms start to sprout across their body, especially their face and airways. On death, they are immediately raised as a [Spore Servant](https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/5195138-myconid-spore-servant)
