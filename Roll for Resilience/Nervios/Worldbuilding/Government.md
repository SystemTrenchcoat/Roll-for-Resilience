Rich Nobles:

- Rich nobles have heavy influence over the government’s decisions
    
- Fewer guard towers in the richer section of the city
    
- Rich nobles pay the government to get away with basically anything and everything
    
- The rich have their own paid off groups of guards/military which are better funded and thus better armed than the standard guards
    
- The rich are the ones behind the secret imports of illegal items
    
- Merrene Family:
    

- Lives on the manor on the small island off the shore of Nervios.
    
- Wealthiest family in the city, controls and regulates trade (formal and underground)
    
- Heavily influences the government, pays off guards and gov’t officials, takes advantage of citizens and spies on them through corrupt guards
    
- Don’t control the tower, but utilize its presence to further instill fear and a feeling of surveillance
    
- Out of direct sightlines of the spire, family referred to as “the unseen” by locals
    
- Double-edged sword: while corrupt, the family provides a plethora of jobs to the citizens and acts as the main source of wealth and income for the city.

Secret Society? (Pupils of the Spire)

- A small group of select elites that know about the inner mechanics and purpose of the spire
    

- Noble family’s heads of household
    
- Significant government or militant leaders
    
- Select engineers(or wizards) that work to maintain the functionality of the spire
    

- Their goal is to keep the townspeople in a constant state of anxiety and fear without overdoing it and causing a revolt
    

- Why?:
    

- People are easier to control.
    
- Negative emotions fuel a dark entity (or maybe fuel the spire itself). Fueling the dark entity keeps the townspeople safe, even though they must live under strict rule
    
- Consolidate power amongst the influential.
    
- Keep the knowledge of the spire hidden.
