A team is provided with three identical small black bags. The bags are labeled A, B, and C. One bag contains a key that opens a prize box. Each bag also contains a written statement underneath. Two of the statements are false, one statement is true.
## Rules:
1. Bags are physically indistinguishable from one another besides their label and statement.
2. Once a bag is opened, the game is over. If the bag contains the key, the team wins and may use the key to open the prize box
3. Bags cannot be peaked inside prior to fully opening
## Statements:
- Bag A: The key is in this bag
- Bag B: The key is not in bag A
- Bac C: The key is not in this bag
## Solution: 
To solve, work through each case.
- If the key is in bag A: 
	- bag A’s statement is true, bag B’s statement is false, bag C’s statement is true
- If the key is in bag B: 
	- bag A’s statement is false, bag B’s statement is true, bag C’s statement is true
- If the key is in bag C:
	- bag A’s statement is false, bag B’s statement is true, bag C’s statement is false  

**Therefore, the key must be in bag C.**
## Prize: 
If the players guess the proper bag, find the key, and unlock the prize box, they are awarded with a small wooden piece of a larger sliding puzzle. The piece looks like an arrow.
