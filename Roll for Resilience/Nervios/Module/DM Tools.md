
*The following is a non-exhaustive list of examples for DM tools and trinkets to help move along the players if they are indecisive or stuck in a situation for longer than desired. 

*Please use these to supplement your creative capabilities rather than limit them. You know your party better than we ever could.

## Magical Movers

### Items and Equipment

- ***Chilly Stopwatch*** - Cold to the touch, this fashionable timeteller lets the user pause time to think and add any extra characters in a short range to its effect. All characters designated to be immune to its effect cannot perform any physical actions to influence anything around them, except the user being allowed to move their hands. Following usage, the item loses charge and must be replenished before you can click it in to freeze time again.
    
- ***Meridian Orb*** - Spherical in shape, this solid but not sturdy reusable artifact allows the party to connect to an otherworldly entity known as the Hearts Honest Merchant. Although their motives are unknown, their bargains are clear; all present, or any that are exclusively addressed, must offer a true answer to any question they request, and you shall be given clear guidance. Questions are unlikely to be benign, and often will force PCs to speak on personal matters or habits.
    
- ***Fallen Star(s)*** - Wish the night sky offered an out to your predicament? Wish Granted! Contrary to the name, activate one of these stars any time of day, speak aloud a physical item, and it will appear. Alternatively, you may use a fallen star to cause a plausible circumstance to come true based on your current surroundings. Regardless, the fallen star dissipates upon usage to only allow for one time.
    
- ***Spyglass / Spectacles of Forwardsight*** - Gazing through the glass allows the user to peer into a 3 dimensional projection of hypothetical near-futures. For best usage of its powers, the user must verbalize and concentrate on the future they wish to see, such as “show me what happens if I pull this lever.” Prolonged or repeated use in a short time frame will cause a crack to form in the glass, which will shatter should one continue. However, an occasional use will simply cause the glass to fog up to convey reaching the end of its momentary limit.
    
- ****Tiny Helper Storage*** - Call it plot convenience, call it divine intervention, this miniature chest always contains another clue to your current affairs, fitting into any bag with near-weightless ease. Unfortunately, anything larger than 6 inches cannot fit into this box of otherwise perfection. Be careful to not store anything you wish to keep inside, there is no guarantee that it will stay once it seals shut after use.
    
- ***Stress Bite(s)*** - Sickly-sweet balls of sugar that give the user a solution to their problem at the price of increasing their Stress, and temporarily inverting the effects of any Stress reducing consumables for a short period of time.
    
- ***Gleeful Glycerin*** - Once primed and at safe distance, players must recall small interactions that made them feel happy or seen to energize a primer pack for these powerful charges. Instructions are attached *Looney Tunes* style on how the glycerin works. When detonated, these charges always create a new way to progress, and should of course be handled with care.
    
- ***Riftlink*** - A crude artifact which establishes a two-way communication rift between the party and an NPC. Does not run out of charge but does require one character to fully concentrate in order to sustain the communication link, leaving them vulnerable, unable to speak, and unable to move. Possible for any non-magic user to keep the rift open as long as they are living and possess at least one hand
    
- ***Mind Knot*** - A dense segment of thread symbiotically bound to its user, which rewards the wearer for announcing their emotions. Once during a short time period, if the wearer has any Stress when they announce their emotion, it will be reduced. However, choosing to not use this item will slowly cause it to grow resentful of its wearer. Being bound to the knot for 2 sessions without using it will force the wearer’s emotions to become a text display on the character at all times, and will no longer reduce Stress.
    
- ***Panic Bracer*** - A band of leather typically worn around the forearm, when the player holds this against their face and focuses, they are guided towards the nearest hiding place from where they currently are. An optional real world gesture to demonstrate using this involves holding your forearm to your forehead

### DM Focused
- ***Emotion check*** - players write down how they are currently feeling on a small piece of paper and…
	- *Public* - must display it in front of themselves for everyone to read
	- *Anonymous* - pass it face down to the DM who will give it to someone else, manifesting in-game as a disembodied whisper that “someone is feeling…”
	- *Private* - hand it to the DM, who is the only one to read it
- Chance of Whimsy, Chance of Havoc - the DM visibly rolls a dice to determine a change to the current room the players are in. Even rolls impart a comforting effect, odds inflict a negative effect.
