# Description
___
As you enter from the [[Rebellion Mural|Rebellion Mural Door]], the air shifts from cool and humid to warm and stuffy. The tunnels here are much larger then the [[Nervios Tunnels|Western Tunnel System]]; the walls colossal and smooth. The ground beneath you has been worn down from years of foot travel and it is clear these paths remain active and busy. Emerald green vines line the walls, woven into an intricate lace pattern. From the vines hang glowing white lotus flowers, lighting the paths ahead. Once each member of the party has entered, the door begins to creak and rumble as it rolls back into place, sealing it yet again. The [[Rebellion Symbol]] is painted onto this side of the door as well, albeit not as grandiosely. 
## Entrance Mural
On the back of the now sealed door, the familiar image of the [[Rebellion Symbol]] is painted for all to see. 
## Inscription
In front of you, directly across from the door, an inscription on the wall reads:
> "***The Boulder a Clock
>  The Moss the Time
>  The Gate Sits at Noon
>  Seek the Broken Hand and Knock Thrice***"

Beneath the inscription, a small patch of blue moss grows on the walls to roughly knee-height. To your left, a path continues on and seems to bend around a corner. To your right, a path leads to a large chamber with a massive boulder in the center.
## Boulder
The boulder is huge and ovular. Its surface smooth and cool to the touch, almost feeling slightly damp. Painted onto the southern edge of the rock is a smaller mural of the [[Rebellion Symbol]]. A distinct pattern of blue, magenta, and yellow moss grows on the skirts between the boulder and the cavern floor. Starting at the symbol and blue moss and traveling clockwise around the rock, the pattern of moss is as follows:
> **Blue** -> yellow -> magenta -> yellow -> **Blue** -> magenta -> yellow -> magenta -> **Blue** -> magenta -> yellow -> yellow -> *Beginning*

Splitting from the boulder chamber, another path leads East, heading farther from the entrance.
## Moss and Tunnels
While travelling around the either path from the entrance, it becomes apparent that these tunnels form a large loop that start and begin at the entrance door. Along the inner walls of the loop, a distinct pattern emerges:
1. First, a large patch of blue moss grows on the inner wall of the loop. 
2. Second, the patch of blue moss is followed by three dead-end tunnels that point in towards the center of the loop. Within these inner-tunnels, exclusively yellow OR magenta moss grow wildly along the walls, floor, and ceiling.
3. Another patch of blue moss grows on the inner wall of the loop.

The pattern continues itself, with three sets of blue moss and three tunnels. Starting at the entrance with the blue moss and travelling clockwise around the loop, the exact pattern is as follows:
> **Blue** -> yellow -> magenta -> yellow -> **Blue** -> magenta -> yellow -> yellow -> **Blue** -> magenta -> yellow -> yellow -> *Beginning*

# Puzzle
___
This looping path is a spatial puzzle that involves comparing patterns and finding the "odd one out". Players must compare the moss pattern around the boulder to the moss pattern around the tunnel loop. 
## Inscription Explanation
- *The Boulder a Clock* - The boulder acts as a tool for determining position and orientation.
- *The Moss the Time* - 12 patches of moss grow around the boulder, and around the loop path alike. They act as the "hour" ticks on a clock
- *The Gate Sits at Noon* - Orients the boulder in regards to the tunnels. "The Gate" is referring to the [[Rebellion Symbol]] painted on both the entrance door, and Southern edge of the boulder.
- *Seek the Broken Hand and Knock Thrice* - The "hands" are the inner-facing tunnels. "Broken" refers to how the color of the moss in this tunnel does not match its respective color on moss around the boulder. Knock thrice literally means to knock three times. In doing so, a hidden door opens up into [[The Burrow]], leading to the next mission: [[Peaking in the Burrow]].

## Comparing Patterns
Starting at the [[Rebellion Symbol]] and headed clockwise around the boulder/loop, a clear pattern emerges. The order of colored moss matches almost exactly one-to-one from the boulder to the loop.
### **Boulder Pattern**:
> **Blue** -> yellow -> magenta -> yellow -> **Blue** -> magenta -> yellow -> magenta -> **Blue** -> magenta -> yellow -> yellow 
### **Tunnel Loop Pattern**:
> **Blue** -> yellow -> magenta -> yellow -> **Blue** -> magenta -> yellow -> *yellow* -> **Blue** -> magenta -> yellow -> yellow 

The tunnel with the *yellow* moss (6th tunnel clockwise from entrance) is yellow while the respective moss on the boulder is magenta. This signifies that this "hand" is broken and the party must knock three times in the tunnel.

## Knocking Thrice
Once players determine the proper path and knock three times within the tunnel, a grating cranking sound echoes around as a hidden door opens up. Once fully open, a gigantic cavern is revealed full of tents, people, and lights. The players have entered [[The Burrow]], the secret underground market of [[Nervios]].
