## Description
A particularly vain tyrant who sees almost everyone as tools to get what she wants. Her upbringing taught her that everything she wants should be delivered to her without hesitation or question, so she fully believes the world belongs to her. There are few people, even among the nobles, that she truly views as equals, and most people utterly detest her.

## Backstory
A particularly vain and stubborn noble who believes she is entitled to the world. Her family has been a part of Nervios since before the towers and were eager to take advantage of the power and protection they bestowed. Kira was always taught that her every wish was to be granted to her and has no tolerance for even the slightest pushback from anyone.
