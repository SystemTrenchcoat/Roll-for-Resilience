Three of the most influential and holy religious leaders of the [[Church of Aequorism]]. They serve as the earthly representations of humankind and are the interpreters of god. Head overseers of religious affairs within the city.
- Moon: The spear of Divinity- [[Caspian Moon]]
	- Holy Representative of God
	- The holy truth and the divine interpreter of gods' word
	- Head of city-wide religious affairs and leader of the sea soothers
- Sea: The spear of Nature- [[Soren Mar]]
	- Head of nature preservation efforts and leader of the Wave 
	- Ensures humanity shows respect and care for the sea.
	- Holy Representative of Nature 
- Sun: The spear of Humanity - [[Isabella Sol]] 
	- Guide Humanity to righteousness
	- Care for all people equally
	- Holy Representative for all of humankind.
