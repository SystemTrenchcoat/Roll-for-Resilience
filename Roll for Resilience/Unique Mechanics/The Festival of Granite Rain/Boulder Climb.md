# Description
On the outskirts of the festival grounds lies a small red tent and a large, steep, man-made hill known as [[Sisyphus Hill]]. At the base of the hill sits a large spherical boulder, nestled into a groove in the ground so it cannot roll away. Outside the red tent stands a small hulking man who stands posing and flexing his muscles. The man, [[Sven]], challenges all to complete the hardest trial of their lives: The Boulder Climb. 

If asked, [[Sven]] is happy to show off his strength and skill by bringing the boulder to to the top single handedly. However, afterwards he will try to get you to sign up for his body building club to improve your strength.

## Rules
1. The boulder must be brought to the top of the steep hill.
2. The boulder climb can be played solo, or in a duo.
3. The boulder can be brought to the top of the hill by any means necessary, as long as the hill and boulder remain intact.
4. To get the boulder at the top of the hill, the challengers must pass a difficult skill check pertaining to their specific solution

**Example Attempts**:
- A team trying to brute force the boulder up the hill by physically pushing it.
- A team tries to pull the boulder up the hill via a rope
- A team uses magic to lift the boulder up the hill
## Prize: 
Upon completion of the boulder climb, the participants are given a [[Granite Puzzle Piece]]; a smaller piece of the larger [[The Wheel of Purity#[The Granite Puzzle](https //puzzel.org/en/slidingpuzzle/play?p=-Ovp593O5-VcfvdPg8Js)|Granite Puzzle]]. The piece contains an image that looks like a “T”
