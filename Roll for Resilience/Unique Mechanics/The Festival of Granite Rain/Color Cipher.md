A team of two approaches a game tent offering a puzzle. Within the tent lies a 5 ft tall beaker of water. The beaker is completely opaque, and the bottom of the water is too dark to see into. The team is provided with a string of multicolored jumbled text, and two clues to help solve the puzzle. The proctor of the puzzle states that the answer is hidden within the text.

Within the confines of the tent lie several bags of various minerals and materials. These include:
- A large bag of soil
- A large bag of fertilizer
- A bag of fish bones
- A large bag of sugar
- A large bag of tea leaves
- A large bag of salt
- A medium bag of fish scales
- A large bag of flower petals
- A small bag of copper fragments

![[ColorCipher.png]]

## Clues
### Clue 1: From sun to sky to ground to sea, these are the colors of which I’ll be.

### Clue 2: The way is led; By purity's grain. Bless thy water with a briny rain

## Solution
**SALT is the answer to the puzzle. The players must dump salt into the beaker of water. Once enough salt is added, a small wooden puzzle piece floats up from the bottom of the beaker. The piece is a smaller fragment of a sliding puzzle and appears as shown below.** 
![[trident_sliding_puzzle_1.jpg]]

**Clue 1 Explained**: Describes the order of colors of the text.  (Yellow --> Blue --> Green --> Blue)

**Clue 2 Explained**: In Nervios, salt is holy and seen as a purifier. Briny rain describes how salt needs to be added to the beaker of water.
