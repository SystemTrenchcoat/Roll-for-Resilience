# Description
Within the stall [[Clara Tea]] lies various boxes, bags, and objects that [[Clara Porter]] has earned while working on the docks at [[Rutmar Port]]. In the center of the stall stands a 5 ft tall beaker of water. The beaker is completely opaque, and the bottom of the water is too dark to see into. In front of the beaker sits a small table, with a torn strip of paper lying on it. Upon inspection, the paper contains a string of jumbled multicolored text.

[[Clara Porter]] challenges those who enter her tent to solve the puzzle that lies before them. She claims that all they need to solve the puzzle stands within the tent. However she enjoys watching people struggle, so she only provides **two** clues. 

The tent has various goods and materials lying around the tent
- Several bags of tea leaves
- A large bag of salt
- A small box labeled "Watery Treasures" filled with miscellaneous Nautical objects
	- Sextant
	- Fishing hooks
	- Unique looking shells and rocks
- Several used fishing rods
- A shelf containing various "rare" sea shells and conchs
- A small bag labeled "mermaid scales"
	- Actually contains fish scales
- A large bag of dirt
- A large bag of manure.

## Clues
1. From sun to sky to ground to sea; in the letters I'm hidden, these colors I'll be.
2. The truth is in order, both in color and placement.
3. The way is led; By purity's grain. Bless thy water with a briny rain

>[!faq]- Clue explanations
>1. The letters that spell SALT are in colored order. The first yellow letter is 'S', representing the color of the sun. 'A' is the first blue letter following the 'S', representing the color of the sky. 'L is the first green letter following 'A', representing the color of the ground. 'T' is the first blue letter following the 'L', representing the color of the sea.
>2. While the text appears jumbled "Salt" is still spelled out in order.
>3. A normal history check would reveal that Salt is considered pure by the [[Church of Aequorism]]. "Briny" refers to salt, and "rain" refers to dumping salt into the beaker.

>[!success]- ## Solution
**SALT is the answer to the puzzle. The players must dump salt into the beaker of water. Once enough salt is added, a small wooden puzzle piece floats up from the bottom of the beaker. The piece is a smaller fragment of a sliding puzzle and appears as shown below.**
