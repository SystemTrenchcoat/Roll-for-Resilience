

- ## Oppressed (Urchin)
	- Scrappy. You see treasure where others see trash. Gain advantage on checks to utilize discarded goods.

- ## Merchant
	- Silver-Tongue. You have a certain way with words. Gain advantage on checks to convince or hold attention of others.

- ## Official
	- Connections. Be you a civil servant or someone higher up, you are of relative importance to the city. You can use your position to gain advantage on making meetings and manipulating people

- ## Tourist
	- **Intrigue**. For one reason or another, you have traveled to [[Nervios]]. Your unique nature grants advantage on rolls to charm.

- ## Fisher
	- **Seafarer**. Your life on the sea has brought certain advantages. You have advantage on rolls relating to fishing and boating.
