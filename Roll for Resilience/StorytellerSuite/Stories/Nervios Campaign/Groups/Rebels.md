# Description
___
The Rebellion is a secret yet expansive group that seeks freedom and liberty for all. They are ashamed of how the great city of [[Nervios]] has fallen, and have organized into an oppositional force targeting the main governing powers of Nervios: [[Guards|The Guards]], [[Church of Aequorism| The Church of Aequorism]], and a number of Noble families who have clung to power through wealth and connections. While they lack the strength to face these powers head on, the Rebels have spent a great amount of time behind the scenes. They believe that great change can come through many quiet actions. These actions include espionage, infiltration, manipulation, granting asylum, and illegal emigration.

## Values and Beliefs
### **1. Work in the Shadows**
Rebels believe that their work is best done in silence and shadows. They mostly operate behind the scenes in hopes of bringing reform and change. They gather intelligence, manipulate politics, and seek violence as a last resort. However, when violence *is* necessary, they are brutal and efficient as to avoid detection. During combat engagements, Rebels utilize guerrilla warfare and their mastery of their environment to devastate their opponents.

Furthermore, the Rebellion has close ties to the criminal underground of Nervios, more specifically [[The Burrow]]. They cooperate and engage with criminals in order to further their goals.
### **2. Equality**
Members of the Rebellion believe that all people, regardless of social status, should have access to basic liberties and rights. The current government and power system in [[Nervios]] strips citizens of these basic rights and forces them to live under an oppressive rule.
### **3. Free Markets and Deregulation**
The Rebellion believes that the market should be free and less-regulated. Currently, the [[Nervios]] markets are highly controlled; only specific exports/imports are allowed past the city walls. This consolidates wealth amongst those who create the rules, and leaves little room for small business-owners to flourish. The desire for free markets is part of the reason that the Rebellion is so involved with [[The Burrow]], the black market of Nervios.
## Goals
1. Free the people from the tyranny of the Guards.
2. Protect the innocent and ensure equality for all
3. Restrict the abilities of the powerful

# Structure
___
## Central Command
The group in charge that directs and oversees rebellion forces. Their ranks are highly confidential-- known only by those within central command and other major Rebel leaders.
### Rangers
The main "fighting" force of the Rebellion. They are masters of stealth and understand the ins and outs of [[Nervios]]. They are tasked with the surveillance of guard routes, movement of dissidents and political refugees, and navigation of the underground [[Nervios Tunnels|Tunnel System]].
### Merchants
Former black-market merchants that have proved loyalty and displayed high-levels of skill have been hand-picked to work for the Rebellion. They help run [[The Burrow]], smuggle illicit goods for the Rebellion, and work with Rangers to sneak dissidents out through exporting ships. These merchants are masters of smuggling and have often spent years sneaking contraband right under the noses of the [[Guards]].
### Moles
Moles are highly-confidential members of the Rebellion tasked with espionage and double-agency. Their main target of espionage is the Guards and Noble Families who run [[Nervios]]. Each mole has a representative Ranger by which they report to. Trusted moles are often tasked with recruiting new double-agents from inside the governing bodies themselves.
# History
___
Rebels have existed in Nervios since the rise of the [[Guards]], yet on a much smaller and less-connected scale. Within the last century, many smaller rebellion groups have combined and consolidated to form the infrastructure known today. As liberties and rights were stripped away, the Rebellion grew in strength and numbers.
