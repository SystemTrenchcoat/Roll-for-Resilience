# Description
___
The everyday people of [[Nervios]]. Not engaged in the lives of guards, rebels, or nobles. They live in relatively poor conditions under the iron rule of the [[Guards]] and face trouble on the daily. The commonfolk are frequently monitored and face constant pressure. Their homes are stacked on top of each other and their neighborhoods are woefully overcrowded. Many live impoverished as the great noble families of Nervios exploit them for labor. In fact, the average citizen can be bankrupt by one bad storm, fire, or tax increase.
