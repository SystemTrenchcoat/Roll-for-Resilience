## Description
___ 
The Guards of [[Nervios]] are a strong authoritarian force that protects the city both from enemies within and without. They are in control of who comes and goes, what goods are exported/imported, and what knowledge from outside the walls is shared within. The Guards are responsible for isolating [[Nervios]] from the outside world, and for creating a surveillance state that watches over all citizens.

## Watch Towers
Stationed in key areas around [[Nervios]], several tall watchtowers dominate the skyline and watch over the streets below. These towers cannot be seen into from outside, which creates a constant feeling of surveillance and a panoptical effect.

## [[The Nervios Eye]]
One watchtower substantially dwarves all others. Nicknamed *The Nervios Eye* by locals, this behemoth of a tower stands proudly in the center of the bay near [[Rutmar Port]]. It is used by the Guards to get a sweeping view of the city, as well as the surrounding terrain. The tower can be seen from anywhere in [[Nervios]], except for a few dark alleyways.

## Corruption
Many members of the City Guard have learned to abuse their power and have fallen to corruption. Some work for the Noble Families, some work for the [[Church of Aequorism]], and some work for themselves or the highest bidder. These self-serving guards worsen the entire public perception of the City Guard, and have led to the Guards being generally feared and resented. Not all Guards are corrupt however; many still wish to protect an uphold the innocent.

# Structure
___
## City Guard
The main force of the Guard focuses and operates *within* the wall of [[Nervios]]. Their forces are split amongst three main ranks:
### 1. Junior
The lowest rank of the Guards. They are trusted with the most basic of tasks and have yet to prove their loyalty and devotion to the cause. They often are seen protecting the peace, enforcing petty laws, making simple arrests, etc.
### 2. Senior
Seniors are respected within their ranks. They have served for some time within the City Guard and have proven their worth and loyalty. They are tasked with more significant roles then Juniors, such as: protecting Noble Families, keeping watch in the Watch Towers, and guarding clergymen of the [[Church of Aequorism]], especially during criminal trials.
### 3. Captain
Captains are the leaders of both Juniors and Seniors. They organize major meetings, design patrol routes, handle recruitment, and decide lower guard scheduling and tasks. Captains are the most trusted members of the Guard, and are often fitted with a [[Gauntlet of Truth]].

## Watchman
The Watchman are a small and specialized group of guards who watch the city walls and port from external threats. They are the deciders of who and what enters and exits [[Nervios]]. Watchman meticulously screen any and all shipping containers and vessels for illicit goods or "criminals" trying to leave.

# History
___
150 years ago the Great City Gate of Nervios was sealed by the City Guard during a time of severe economic turmoil. Integral trade routes had been shut off by a war between neighboring cities, creating a food and coin draught within Nervios. Struggling to survive, the great people of Nervios quickly started coming for each others throats. Many starved. Many fled. Many rioted. To keep the peace the guards were forced to seal the city off from the outside world and become an independent city state. 

Slowly but surely relief returned to the Nervios people. The city became majorly self-sufficient, minus a few necessary imports and exports to keep currency flowing. Watchtowers began sprouting throughout the inner districts of the city, creating a feeling of protection at the cost of privacy. The largest watchtower was built in the center of the large bay, casting a watchful eye on those who dare threaten the City Walls. For a short period, life was peaceful and calm for the Nerviosi populace. 

The peace however, only lasted so long. The city guard, once sworn to protect the people, begun growing corrupt and self-serving. At the same time, the old religion [[Sea Scribes of Umorism|Umorism]] had been replaced by the [[Church of Aequorism]] which centered around conformity and following Gods word to a tee. Noble families also began bribing and influencing members of the guard to do their personal bidding. 

As the powerful grew stronger, the daily man suffered. The watchtowers, once a symbol of protection, became the icon for observation and authority. Those who broke even the lightest of laws faced harsh punishments and unfair trials. Life for the commonfolk became one of silence and conformity, but even their silence wasn't enough after the invention of the [[Gauntlet of Truth]], a tool used by the guards to peer into the minds of the unknowing. 

Today, the daily people of Nervios find a new kind of peace. They are impoverished, crowded, and stripped of their privacy, yet this is all they've ever known. They still manage to live happy and full lives, even while stripped of many liberties. For the especially misfortunate, whisperings of a [[Rebels|rebellion group]] bring hope and a future. Corruption still plagues the guards, albeit much less then before. Many new recruits wish to serve out of tradition and honor, rather than greed or power.
