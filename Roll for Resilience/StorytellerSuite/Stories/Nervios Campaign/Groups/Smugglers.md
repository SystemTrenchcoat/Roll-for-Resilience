## Description

The smugglers run the Burrow, supplying the city of Nerivos with the majority of their illegal goods. They have close ties with the Rebels and Sea Scribes. Both of these groups need the Burrow to avoid the guards, while the Smugglers need the rebels for more man power and the Sea Scribes to help create more advance ships to smuggle more stuff.
