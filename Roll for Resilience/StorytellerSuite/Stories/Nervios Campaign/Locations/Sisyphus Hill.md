## Description
A small tent with a red canvas cover sits next to a steep man made hill. The booth is occupied by a small gnomish body builder named [[Sven]]. Sven invites players to work by themselves or in small groups to push a large boulder to the top of his hill. In return they will earn a key [[Granite Puzzle Piece]] necessary to solve the [[The Wheel of Purity#The Granite Puzzle|The Granite Puzzle]] 
## History
Each year, Sven personally builds and demolishes the steep hill for this game.
