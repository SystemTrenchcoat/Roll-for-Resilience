## Description
- Village that lies right beside the port, facing the seaside
    
- Used to be primarily a small fishing village, but once Rutmar Port was built, the fishermen were driven away
    
- Now, it primarily consists of lodge owners who make revenue by housing merchants and outlanders
    
- The guards took the town under government control to monitor outlanders and hired the lodge owners as “sellouts”, in a sense
    
- Only select few outlanders are allowed to stay beyond the town boundary and must be accompanied by a guard at all times
