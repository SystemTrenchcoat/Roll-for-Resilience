## Description
In the center of the bay of [[Nervios]], a large spire towers over the city below. The foundation of the spire is wide and tall, and sits upon a small island made of sharp rocks and boulders. During high-tide, the island completely submerges under the bay, and the bottom of the tower gets hammered by the crashing waves of the ocean. As your eye follow from the bottom of the tower to the top, a sense of gloom overtakes you. This structure is larger than anything you've seen before. It is larger then the grand cathedral, taller then the great walls that surround the city and gargantuan even compared to the guard towers that loom over the streets. A top the structure rests a massive ornate eye made of gold and silver. The eye is tangible and real, yet its presence creates a feeling of constant surveillance. The pupil of the eye is said to follow you, no matter where you go. Whether within the city walls, or out at sea, you feel as you are being watched.
## History
This large spire was the first tower of observation that the guards built when they began using them shortly after coming into power. It is said that powerful magics are housed within the tower that allows them to keep constant vigilance over the only exit of the city; the docks.
