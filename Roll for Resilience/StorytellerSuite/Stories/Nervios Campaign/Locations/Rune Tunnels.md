## Description
A series of interconnected tunnels and chambers that lead to a room with 6 chests. To navigate the tunnels properly, view the [[Rune Puzzle]].

Upon entering the first chamber, it is apparent that none of the natural fauna from the rest of the tunnels grow here. These tunnels are incredibly dark, with the only lighting emanating faintly from three distinct runes on the ground. The runes stand at the foot of three branching tunnel paths that split out from from the chamber. 

## History
This series of tunnels and chambers are naturally formed and have been turned into a test by the [[Rebels]].

A difficult arcane check can reveal that the runes on the ground are magical in some sense.
