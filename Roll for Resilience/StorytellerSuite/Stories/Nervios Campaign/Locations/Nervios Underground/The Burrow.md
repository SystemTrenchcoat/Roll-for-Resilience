## Description
The underground market of [[Nervios]], run by the [[Smugglers]] that supplies the city of [[Nervios]] with most of its illegal goods, while also providing a space for various citizens to intermingle without the fear of the Eye or the [[Guards]]. The [[Rebels]] and [[Sea Scribes of Umorism]] also use the Burrow as a base of operations, assisting the [[Smugglers]] in maintaining it.

The burrow is categorized by being lit by many bioluminescent plants that are within the hanging plants, personalized masks almost all people wear, and the low but lax whispers words are spoken in.
