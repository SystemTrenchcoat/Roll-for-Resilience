## Description
A series of wide open tunnels that run under the eastern side of the [[Nervios]] peninsula. The tunnels surround [[The Burrow]] and eventually connect to a crypt underneath the [[Church of Aequorism|Aequor]] Cathedral.

These tunnels appear much cleaner and more active than the previous tunnel systems on the other side of the [[Rebellion Mural|Rebellion Mural Door]]. The ground is smooth and free of clutter, and visible footpaths are worn into the ground from years of frequent use. Glowing moss lines the edges of the cave, providing light and visibility for travelers within. Your shoulders drop as the space becomes much more open as it was was previously 

On the wall straight across from the entrance is an inscription that reads:
> "***The Boulder Your Compass
> The Moss Your Guide***"

To your left, the tunnel continues and bends around a corner. To your right, a large boulder sits the center of an open chamber. Several plants from the [[Flower Puzzle]] line the ground bordering the boulder. 
## History
These tunnels see a lot more life then the western tunnels. Signs of human intervention exist throughout the system.
