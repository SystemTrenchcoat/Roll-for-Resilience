## Description
A series of wide open tunnels that run under the eastern side of the [[Nervios]] peninsula. The tunnels surround [[The Burrow]] and eventually connect to a crypt underneath the [[Church of Aequorism|Aequor]] Cathedral

## History
These tunnels see a lot more life then the western tunnels. Signs of human intervention exist throughout the system.
