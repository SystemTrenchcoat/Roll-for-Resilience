## Description
As the party travels deeper through the southern tunnels from [[Central Cavern]], the air begins to feel electric and almost alive, and the walls grow ever more cramped. After a short time of following the path in the dark, a faint blue glow can be seen up ahead.

As you approach the light, the tunnel opens up into a small chamber with high ceilings and stalagmites. On the ground at your feet, it is revealed that the glow is coming from three distinct magical runes on the ground. On the walls behind the runes are three respective doorways, where the path ahead seems to ascend vertically. Despite the glow of the runes, the paths appear pitch-black with no way of seeing where they lead without travelling down them.

## History
This series of tunnels and chambers are naturally formed and have been turned into a test by the [[Rebels]].

> [!info] A difficult arcane check can reveal that the runes on the ground are magical.
