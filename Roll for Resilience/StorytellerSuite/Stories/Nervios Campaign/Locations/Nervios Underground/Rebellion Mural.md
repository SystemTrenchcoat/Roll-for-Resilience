## Description
A small cavern with a large painted mural of the [[Rebellion Symbol]]. Next to the mural are two torches, which are lit and burning despite the cavern being desolate of people.

## History
The paint of the mural seems to be aging with time. An investigation will reveal the mural has been here for at least two decades.
