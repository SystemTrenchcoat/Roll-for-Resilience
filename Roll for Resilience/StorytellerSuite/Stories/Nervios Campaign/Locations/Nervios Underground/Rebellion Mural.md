## Description
At the end of the Eastern Tunnels from [[Central Cavern]] exists a large open cavern with high ceilings and jagged walls. The air here feels damp, with a distinct smell of saltwater. A small pond sits in the center of the cavern, exuding an ethereal glow. Two lit torches hang on the far wall from the entrance, with a massive painted mural between them. As your eyes adjust to the new lighting, the mural begins to take shape: the [[Nervios]] city gate, once sealed with boulders and rubble, now open with the boulders divided down the center.
![[rebellionSymbol.png|300]]
Several lotus flowers grow in the water along the South-Eastern edge of the pond. Upon closer inspection, the flowers are actually stone sculptures of the flowers, placed in an almost-symmetrical arch pattern. The left side of the arch is visibly missing one lotus flower. A deep cylindrical notch is carved into the ground where the flower should be.

>[!info] Investigation Checks
>- If the player characters hold connections to [[The Burrow]] or the [[Rebels]], a normal difficulty check reveals the mural as [[Rebellion Symbol|The Symbol of the Rebellion]]. 
>- If the player characters have earned both halves of the [[Stone Lotus Flower]] from the [[Rune Puzzle]] and [[Flower Puzzle]], they will recognize the sculpture to be the same as the flowers in the pond. Inserting the flower into the notch unlocks the mural door.
>- If the player characters investigate the mural, a difficult check reveals that the mural is painted on a large circular door made of rock; it's creases filled with dirt and dust, blending in with the surrounding wall. With this information, players could find a way to dig through or destroy the door.

>[!success]- ## Unlocking the Mural Door
> Both halves of the [[Stone Lotus Flower]] must be collected, combined, and inserted into the notch in the pond to unlock the door. The lotus bud and stem are found from the [[Flower Puzzle]] in the [[Flora Tunnels]], and the [[Rune Puzzle]] in the [[Rune Tunnels]] respectively.
> ___
> Once the [[Stone Lotus Flower]] has been inserted into the notch, the closed bud of the flower begins to open and blossom. At the same time, a deep rumble is heard as the mural wall begins shaking. Slowly, dust and dirt begin to pour out in a circle around the mural, revealing the mural is painted onto a massive circular door. The door slowly sinks back into the wall, then rolls out of way, revealing a path ahead towards the [[Eastern Tunnel System]].
## History
The paint of the mural seems to be aging with time. An investigation can reveal the mural has been here for at least two decades.
