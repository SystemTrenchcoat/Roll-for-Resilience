# Description
___
## Scene Description
A large and open underground cavern with smooth walls and a jagged ceiling riddled with stalagmites. On the outer walls of the space, several tunnels branch off into darkness. The cavern is *dim*, lit only by the faint glow of the glow moss that lines the walls and floors of the space. In the center of the cavern lies a large pile of stone and debris from the collapse of [[Central Stage]]. These boulders that were used to create the stage now seal the sinkhole that brought the party here. Furthermore, additional debris from the [[The Festival of Granite Rain]] lie scattered around the bounds of the cavern. 

The air is cool with a stale humidity to it; the smell of damp rock and clay fill the space. The cavern is quiet. The only sounds are the distant dripping of water and the echoes from your own footsteps. Despite the terrifying and sudden scene of the stage collapse, the cavern brings an almost eerie tranquility. Down here, you are free from the social and cultural pressures of the city above.

## Tunnels
Along the outer walls of the cavern, four large tunnels split off into the darkness. Their paths shielded by a thick veil of shadow. 

>[!info] Passive Perception Check
> As the party's eyes adjust to the extremely dark environment, a *moderate* passive perception check reveals a flickering orange light coming from the South-Eastern most tunnel. Travelling down this tunnel brings the party to [[Rebellion Mural]]

The four tunnels eventually converge towards three major locations. The first tunnel traveled should always leads toward the [[Rebellion Mural]].
### 1. [[Rebellion Mural]]
The most important location for the party to visit. A large open cavern with a pond and a large painted mural of the [[Rebellion Symbol]]. The mural acts as a clue to come back to once completing the puzzles in the other two locations.
### 2. [[Flora Tunnels]]
Rewards the party with the [[Stone Lotus Flower#Bud|Closed Bud of the Stone Lotus Flower]], which is half of a key that unlocks the hidden door at the [[Rebellion Mural]]
### 3. [[Rune Tunnels]]
Rewards the party with the [[Stone Lotus Flower#Inner Bulb|Inner Bulb of the Stone Lotus Flower]], which is the other half of a key that unlocks the hidden door at the [[Rebellion Mural]]
