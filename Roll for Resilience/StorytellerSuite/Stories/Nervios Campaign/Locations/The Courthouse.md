# Description
___
The courthouse is a well known and highly active part of [[Nervios]]. Trials occur near-daily and crowds of curious and nosy citizens flock to the viewing windows to get a peek of the proceedings inside. It is an old structure, built with tall walls of stone brick layered with elegant decorative stone arches that hint at a more architecturally innovative time. It's massive oak doors are thick and weathered, creaking loudly whenever they are heaved open or shut. Along the lower walls, large glass viewing windows are set between the stonework, with long wooden benches positioned before them so the public can observe court hearings from outside.
