## Description
The main tunnels open up into a small cavern, where three doorways sit on the wall to left to the entrance. In front of the far wall exists three ornate pedestals, each with a different plant atop of them. 

## History
Upon closer inspection, the three doorways seem man-made, juxtaposing the natural formation of the rest of the tunnels and caverns.
