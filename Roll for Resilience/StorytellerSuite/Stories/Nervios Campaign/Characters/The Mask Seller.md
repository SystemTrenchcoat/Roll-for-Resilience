## Description
The Burrow's mask seller, who sells masks to new visitors to the Burrow and provides cosmetic adjustments to the masks of familiar voices of the Burrow. They speak in a low, calm voice that furnishes the conversation with flowery language.

## Example Dialogue
- "How may I help you this fine evening?"
- "Care for a mask? They allow one to hide their external features while letting the inner ones shine through."
- "A trip back to the surface can be arranged, but not by these hands of mine."

## Backstory
Not much is known about the mask seller, they've been a long standing member for the Burrow since near its inception. They showed up one day, offering masks. They are seen as the loveable mystery of [[The Burrow]].


>[!faq]- Secret
>Though the players may be warry of the Mask Seller, they are a genuine nice person that just likes to play the role of a cryptic elder. Whether or not they are an elder is up for debate.
