## Description
An honorable man who works as a guard in order to protect the peace. He believes in justice and impartiality and is willing to do what it takes to uphold the law. Other guards know not to act mischievously or criminally around him. 
### Physical Description
Adam is a tall strong man with chiseled features and long slicked-back golden-brown hair. He stands tall with dignity and pride.
## Backstory
Adam comes from a family of merchants and was raised to be an honest and good man. One day he discovered his family was active in the black market and had been lying to him his entire life.  Broken, Adam reported his family to the guards and pledged his life to truth, justice, and honor.
