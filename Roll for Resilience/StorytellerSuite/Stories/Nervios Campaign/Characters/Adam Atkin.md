## Description
An honorable man who works as a guard in order to protect the peace. He believes in justice and impartiallity and is willing to do what it takes to uphold the law. Other guards know not to act mischeviously around him. 

## Backstory
Adam comes from a family of merchants and was raised to be an honest and good man. One day he discovered his family was active in the black market and had been lying to him his entire life.  Broken, Adam reported his family to the guards and pledged his life to truth, justice, and honor.
