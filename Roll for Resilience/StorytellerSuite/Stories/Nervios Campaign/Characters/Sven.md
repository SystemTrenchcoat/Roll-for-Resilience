## Description
A gnomish body builder with large rippling muscles, too big for his small frame. He has a body building beach bro attitude. He takes any opportunity to flex his strength, and will often showboat during his own game, [[Boulder Climb]], to prove he is stronger than everyone. 

## Backstory
Sven has a girlfriend, and no kids. He focuses more on his gains and outward appearance more than starting a family or forming strong interpersonal relationships.
