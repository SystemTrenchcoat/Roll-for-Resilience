## Description
A gnomish body builder with large rippling muscles, too big for his small frame. He has a body building beach bro attitude. He takes any opportunity to flex his strength, and will often showboat during his own game, [[Boulder Climb]], to prove he is stronger than everyone. It doesn't take much to realize his build is not natural.

## Backstory
Sven has a short-term girlfriend, and no kids. He focuses more on his gains and outward appearance more than starting a family or forming strong interpersonal relationships. He recently started a body improvement club, where he sells his homemade "[[Muscle Milk]]". Unfortunately for Sven, he often scares off potential members before they sign on.
