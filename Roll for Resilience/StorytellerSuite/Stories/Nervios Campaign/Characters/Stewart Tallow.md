## Description
A rebellion ranger and a man of few words. Highly respected amongst his ranks, he is known as a master of stealth. 

## Backstory
Stewart has been a part of the rebellion for nearly a decade. He has a perfect track record as a ranger, yet few know much about him. He joined the rebellion after corrupt guards burned down his butcher shop and robbed him for all he was worth. He currently has a wife and daughter, but it has been a couple of years since he last saw them.
