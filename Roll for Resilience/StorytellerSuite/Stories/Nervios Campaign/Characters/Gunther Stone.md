## Description
A nearly middle-aged dwarf who enjoys providing healthy meals for his community. His attempts to sell authentic dwarven food on the surface usually fail because said cuisine uses rocks and for the mostly human population of Nervios, its at best inedible or at worst, deadly. However, his stall in [[The Burrow]] is by far more successful, running the Food Pit down there.

## Backstory
Gunther has lived in the city of Nervios his entire life. He has a wife and one daughter. He opened Fine Pebbles so he could provide a healthy dining spot for local dwarves.
