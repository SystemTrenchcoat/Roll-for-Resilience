## Description
A heroic and brave man who joined the rebellion to fight against the tyranny of the guards.

## Backstory
Tassos grew up an underdog and the runt of his house. As he matured, he became aware and angered by the corruption of the guards and the constant surveillance of the commonfolk. When the time came to move out and join the workforce, Tassos instead joined the rebellion and dedicated himself to pursuing freedom and joy for all.

## # Physical Appearance
Despite his youthful appearance, Tassos' presence commands respect and honor. His emerald green eyes appear wise and knowing and his shimmering white smile brings comfort to those nearby. His golden hair is of medium length and is parted down the middle. He has a full and maintained beard with a scar traveling from his left cheekbone down to the center of his chin.
