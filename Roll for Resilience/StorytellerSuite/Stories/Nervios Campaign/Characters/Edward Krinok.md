## Backstory
A native to [[Nervios]], Edward has never known true freedom. His days were haunted by the relentless eyes that watched over the entire city, just like everyone else. He was the same way when he joined the guards, terrified by the thought of making the smallest mistake, until inevitably, he did. It started off innocent, messed up paperwork, wrong patrol route, that kinda thing, but every time, his fellow guards covered for him. He learned that the guard *is* the true power of [[Nervios]] and started using his to the fullest. Forcing people to clear out of places he wanted to go, harassing and stealing from other citizens, anything he could to feed his power trip.

## Motivations
To keep gaining more power and status. To be so big that no one in [[Nervios]] could ever interfere with him.
