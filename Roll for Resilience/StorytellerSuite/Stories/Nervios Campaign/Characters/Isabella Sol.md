## Description
A devoted and seasoned priestess who cares for all people. Her love for all life has earned her the spot of Spearhead of Sun of the High Trident. She has dedicated her life to helping those in need and is known as a pillar of her community and her religion.

## Backstory
Isabella has devoted her life to helping those in need. She has commandeered great respect amongst guards, commonfolk, and rebels alike and is a beacon of hope and support within the community. She has few earthly possesions and rarely asks for tithes, instead asking that those she helps remain devout and pious.
