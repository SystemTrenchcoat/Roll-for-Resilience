## Description
The Divine Prosecutor for the Church of Aequorin. Believes that all mortals are guilty under the eye of god.
### Physical Description
Iris has fair skin and platinum blond hair to match. Her face is full of freckles and portrays a sense of youth that her strict persona cannot.  She wears a golden intricate tiara and long flowing white robes.
## Backstory
Iris lives a life dedicated to worship and Aequorin. She has no family, few material possessions, and prays twice daily. She has been the Divine Prosecutor for 38 years and has won ~ 60% of her cases. She is young for an elf, and has yet to develop the maturity needed for such a significant role.
