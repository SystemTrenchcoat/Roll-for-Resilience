(LI-NET) (she/her)

Strongly advise against calling her Lyn. Only one person who does still lives, because patricide is frowned upon.

## Physical Description
Blossoming locks of brown hair falling past her shoulders, decorating the slightly muscular tone of her slender frame. Her hazel eyes are complimented by a scar laying across her cheek, a trophy she holds over her siblings for having the courage to actually get her hands dirty.

## Backstory
From a young age, Lynette was eager to get herself involved in the family business dealings, more than she should have at the time a few thought. When she turned 15, she got her first indirect taste of their business, chasing that high until she went on her first dealing solo at 17. One of these solo business affairs was responsible for the scar across her face, a fact she did not let deter her for the following two years she continued. She finally resigned herself to a more indirect position at 23, where she remains until now.
