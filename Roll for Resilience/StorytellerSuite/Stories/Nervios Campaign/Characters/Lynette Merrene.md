(LI-NET) (she/her)

Strongly advise against calling her Lyn. Only one person who does still lives, because patricide is frowned upon.

## Physical Description
Blossoming locks of brown hair falling past her shoulders, decorating the slight musculature of her 5'2" slender body. Her hazel eyes are complimented by a scar laying across her cheek, a trophy she holds over her siblings for having the courage to actually get her hands dirty.

Players with 13 passive wisdom / perception will notice
>*Poking through the right end of her top is an ornate dagger sheath, barely visible to the inattentive eye yet hardly obstructed. You sense it would be wise for her to keep it there*

## Vocal Performance
Lynette speaks with rather succinct pronunciation, not lingering on the sound of any word, even while thinking on the proper word to use next. She does not use flowery language often, choosing simpler words and adding more for emphasis rather than multiple syllables. 

## Sample Dialog
- *I've made myself clear. All that remains is your end*
- *What, this little trophy? Please, my brothers should be the only ones impressed by it*
- *Do as I ask, and you will see payment, I swear to it.*

## Backstory
From a young age, Lynette was eager to get herself involved in the family business dealings, more than she should have at the time a few thought. When she turned 15, she got her first indirect taste of their business, chasing that high until she went on her first dealing solo at 17. One of these solo business affairs was responsible for the scar across her face, a fact she did not let deter her for the following two years she continued. She finally resigned herself to a more indirect position at 23, where she remains until now.
