## Description
A good man who wishes to stop the heinous acts of the rebellion. While aware of the corruption amongst his ranks, he believes the rebellion is a greater evil. Has a wife of three years and a one-year old baby.
### Physical Description
Eagan is a plain looking man with short brown hair and a missing front tooth.  Despite his average looks, he is muscular and visibly strong under his guard uniform.

## Backstory
When Eagan was a kid, he was saved by guards during a housefire that was inadvertently caused by rebels. To Eagan, the guards became a symbol of hope and honor while the rebellion became symbols of fear and criminality.
