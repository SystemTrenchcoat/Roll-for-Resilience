## Description
Gweneth is a Sea Scribe of Umorism, one of the oldest in the city. She spends most of her time in either [[The Burrow]] or [[Rutmar Port]] late at night where few can see her secret tending of [[Smugglers]]' vessels to install the secret compartments necessary for illegal items to avoid detection. She spends most of her time teaching smugglers and others in [[The Burrow]] how to read the stars and tell the temperament of the sea. They talk in a curt manner, preferring to dispense wisdom as few words as possible
