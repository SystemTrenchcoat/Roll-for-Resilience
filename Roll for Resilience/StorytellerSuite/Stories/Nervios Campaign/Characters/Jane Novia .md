## Description
Jane is a short, petite woman with long golden hair as straight as silk.  Despite her small frame, soft voice, and calm appearance, Jane is renowned for her short-temper. She speaks without a filter and often finds herself the center of attention because of it.

## Backstory
As a descendant of the former noble family of [[Nervios]], Jane has lived a pampered and luxurious lifestyle. However, despite her coddled past, Jane is a bit of a misfit. Under the "princess" exterior lies an aggressive and entitled beast with a bit of a superiority complex. Due to previous incidents, her family has put her under the watch of the guards-- both to protect herself and those around her, and to ensure familial secrets don't slip.
