## Description
A former noble who has joined the rebellion after her family's power, wealth, and title were stripped by rival families. She serves as a key member of the rebellion and was a main informant on the inner workings of Nerviosi politics. 

### Physical Appearance
Eleni is a tall slender woman with short blond hair. Her face and ears are pierced with intricate gold and silver jewelry and she wears long, maroon silk robes. Her fancy clothes are reminiscent of her noble and wealthy past.

## Backstory
Eleni comes from former noble family, The Dorians, who were the major importers of specialized textiles within Nervios. She was raised with the expectation that when her father passed, she would become the family lead. Unfortunately for Eleni, rival families took advantage of her fathers passing and did everything in their power to destroy her family's identity and structure. Furious at being forced out of the nobles, Eleni pledged herself to the rebels and has remained an active member for nearly a decade. She is a key part of the rebellion due to her former position and knowledge of the inner workings of Nervios.
