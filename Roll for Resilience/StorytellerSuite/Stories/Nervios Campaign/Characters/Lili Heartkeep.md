## Physical Description
Her spiritual form is draped in the pink tunic she favored during life, which tenderly hugs at her pudgy belly and stout arms. Gracefully flowing down her back are strands of silver hair, the well-kept pride of her appearance parted down the center.

## Vocal Performance
Mrs. Heartkeep has a slower paced voice, getting out around two to three syllables every second, carrying the warm tones of an old mother caring for young children. Her voice is sweet, if not infantilizing to some despite meaning well.

## Sample Dialog
- *Dearie, could you do an old lady a favor?*
- *When the light came back for me, I thought it was time. No, no this isn't right.*
- *Now's not the time to dawdle, sweetie. You still need to free me.*
- *Back into the cold darkness, at last....bless you, children.*

## Backstory
Over several wonderful years, she mothered 4 children, coddling them until they could stand on their own. Her gentle temperament in their early years was hardened by discipline during their growth into adulthood. She died peacefully in her sleep one night, happily awaiting what the next life would bring her. 

Unfortunately, she's been prevented from seeing this because her body is being used by a necromancer. Because of this, she pleads with any who would lend her an ear to assist her. Lili was the first of the "fresher" corpses that [[Nysaphia]] snuck away from the crypts that the city still actively uses.
