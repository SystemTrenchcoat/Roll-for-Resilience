## Description
One of the most prominent black market dealers in the Burrow, Bethia sells various things like recreational drugs, exotic goods, banned books, and cheap food that she sells at a much smaller profit margin than that of any official merchant top side would.

## Backstory
She started out as a minor merchant, till she eventually met a Sea Scribe who taught her ways to better construct her ships. This lead to her being able to develop a great method to help her start smuggling in various goods from aborad, leading to her settin up the Burrow and creating the cureent smuggling ring.
