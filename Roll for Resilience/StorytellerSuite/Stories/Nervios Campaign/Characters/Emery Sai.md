(EM-AIR-REE SIGH) - (any)
As one of the last living members of his family, he carries the long legacy of Umorist scribes upon his shoulders, coming to embody the dimming flame of the faith as a whole. He's determined to enshrine the memory of Umorism somewhere safe, as his final religious undertaking.

## Description
A black braid dangles down the back of an eerily smooth and slender frame. Dancing down the side length of his neck is a gash of scarred tissue, averting your gaze from his amber eyes.

Emery speaks in a drawn out and breathy manner, with most sounds coming from the front of the mouth. 

# Sample Dialog
- *It brings me hope to know some are willing to coexist*
- *May the sea's splendor bless your travels*
- *My papa told me stories of the simpler times, when we needn't hide away to survive.*
- *Let not my complexion fool you, I hold more experience than most think*

## Backstory
Emery was brought into a devout family from a young age, who happily preached Umorism into his impressionable young mind. 

Despite the many tests and persecution he has suffered from believing in Umorism, he remains faithful.

>[!faq]- Secret
> Emery Sai is merely a long running persona, since before Umorists were forced into hiding. The changeling's true name is Nix, with colorless skin sporting several wrinkles and shoulder-length silver hair, on top of the scar along their neck. 
> 
> While the name is a lie, the story is not. Nix really was raised Umorist, because his family adored the fluidity of change as a key tenet. He used to unshift in his family home, but no longer felt safe enough to do so once the persecution began.
