## Description
A strict, no-nonsense blacksmith who provides armor and weapons to guards in daylight, and to rebels in the night.
### Physical Description
Gregory stands at an average height and average build, but his body shows the signs of a man dedicated to his craft. His hands are tough and seasoned and his eyes shimmer while he works.
## Backstory
Husband to Ruby Marsh and father of two, Gregory works as a blacksmith in the poorer section of the city. His wife acts as an involuntary informant for the rebels, as she provides him with gossip from the guards at her tavern, which he provides to the rebellion. He works for both sides as a way to earn a living, and tries not to meddle too much beyond that.
