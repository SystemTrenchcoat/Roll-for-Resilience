## Description
A tall and towering member of the High Trident and the Spearhead of Divinity. Caspian is completely and utterly devoted to god to the point of nearly becoming a misanthrope. He sees the average man as impure and something that needs to be cleansed.

### Physical Description
Caspian towers over the majority of the city, both physically and figuratively. His hair is sleek and silver; his face clean shaven. His frame is hidden behind long robes of white and gold which flow and waft in an ethereal quality.

## Backstory
Caspain has been a part of the church since he was a newborn. He was born with sleek, silver hair and eyes the color of the moon. He was raised with a divine purpose: to be the Spearhead of the moon of the High Trident. In this position, Caspian lived a life of devotion and worship, completely dedicating himself to god. While an exceptionally holy man, he holds humanity to exceptionally high standards and is often dissapointed. Caspain believes his position in life is strictly due to his piety, thus feeling that everyone is capable of significantly improving their life by devoiting themselves to god.
