## Description
Renowned baker and mother of three. Wants to bring a little bit of joy to everyone through her treats. 

### Physical Appearance
Despite Lucia's small frame, she retains a confident and commanding presence. She wears a large white bandana over the top of her head and and a white apron to match. 

## Backstory
Single mother of three boys that lost her husband at sea. She lives a busy life, working long hours at her bakery in order to provide for her children. Despite being chronically overworked, Lucia loves helping others and takes great pride in her craft.
