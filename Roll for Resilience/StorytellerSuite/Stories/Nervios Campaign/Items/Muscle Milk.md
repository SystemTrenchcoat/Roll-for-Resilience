## Description
A small glass potion full of bubbling milk. Upon closer inspection, the milk seems to be perpetually souring. Not quite curdled, not quite fresh.

## History
Muscle Milk is a proud innovation of [[Sven]] the body builder. It was invented to help members of his club gain muscle mass quickly and easily.

## Cultural Significance
Few people in [[Nervios]] have consumed this beverage. Many are scared off by the bubbling or [[Sven]]s aggressive personality.

## Magic Properties
When consumed, the potion gives a small bonus on all strength rolls and a small penalty on all charisma rolls. The effect lasts for 4 hours.
