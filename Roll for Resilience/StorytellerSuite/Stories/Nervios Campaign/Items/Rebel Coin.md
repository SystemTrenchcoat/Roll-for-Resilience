## Description
A silver coin with an embossed eye in the center. Around the eye lies engraved text that reads "All sins must be washed away in order to see the truth."

## History
The coin itself seems relatively new; free from any major wear and tear.

## Cultural Significance
The eye is a familiar sight in [[Nervios]] and a reminder that the [[Guards]] are always watching. This however, is a red herring used by the [[Rebels]] to avoid suspicion. The text on the coin hints at a puzzle to be solved. [[Rebel Coin Puzzle]].
