## Description
A stone sculpture of a lotus flower made of two independent pieces: a stem and a bud. Once both pieces have been earned and screwed together, they can be used to unlock the hidden mural door at the [[Rebellion Mural]].
### Stem
A long-thin cylindrical stone rod. One end of the rod is threaded, the other end has been carved into a key-like pattern. The stone is cool and smooth to the touch. The stem is earned from the [[Rune Puzzle]] in the [[Rune Tunnels]]
### Bud
A stone sculpture of a closed-lotus flower bulb. It's petals form the shape of a raindrop. A deep threaded hole is carved in the bottom of the bud. The bulb is earned from the [[Flower Puzzle]] in the [[Flower Puzzle]].
## History
Found in two pieces from the [[Flower Puzzle]] and [[Rune Puzzle]]. The craftsmanship is impressive; it is clear countless hours have been put into this sculpture.
