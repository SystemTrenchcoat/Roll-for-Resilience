## Description
A bitter plant with thick leaves that glow slightly blue due to bioluminescence. Eating one would make you sick to your stomach, though you would glow blue for a bit. The intensity and duration of all effects would be based on how much was consumed.

## History
It grows throughout the [[Nervios Tunnels]] and provides a dim light.
