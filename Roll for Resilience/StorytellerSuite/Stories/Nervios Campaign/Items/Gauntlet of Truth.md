# Description
___
## Physical Appearance
A lone gauntlet made of steel and leather. In the palm, an eyeball juts from the center. It seems to have no complex mechanical parts and if investigated, appears to function magically.

## Usage
[[Gauntlet of Truth|Gauntlet's of Truth]] are used by the upper ranks of the guards to peer into the minds of the commonfolk. When shaking hands with another person, the wearer is able to hear the thoughts of the other.

# Mind Reading
___
When wearing the Gauntlet of Truth and shaking hands with a target character, the target should write down three "thoughts" they are having: an active thought, a masking thought, and a secret thought.

## Active Thought
Current goals, motivations, or plans that the character is working towards.
*ex) "I am going to the market to buy bread"*
## Secret Thought
A deep secret the character is hiding.
*ex) "I am a member of the rebellion"*
## Masking Thought
Thoughts that are meant to hide active or secret thoughts.
*ex) "This guard has a weak grip"*

After noting the three thoughts down, both parties make a *contested wisdom check*. If the target wins the contest, they sense you poking around in their mind and you gain no significant information. If you win the contest, refer to the table below.

| Roll Difference | Outcome                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| --------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1 - 4           | **Narrow Win**: You are able to read the Masking Thought of the target. It comes to you in a flurry of whispers, just barely intelligible. As you read the thoughts of the target, they feel your presence within their mind. |
| 5-9             | **Clear Win**: You are able to read the Active Thought of the target. You hear their voice clearly within your head. The target feels a slight itch in their mind.                                                            |
| 10+             | **Overwhelming Win**: You are able to to peer deeply into the mind of the target and read their Secret Thought. Their secret booms within your head while the target senses nothing.                                          |
