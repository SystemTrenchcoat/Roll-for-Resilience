## Description
A healthy dining option for dwarves. Made from fresh oysters, fruit, and ground stone(pebbles). A beloved meal by dwarves, yet a dangerous one for humans. 

The dish is often served with a side or drizzle of "Gunther's special sauce", which is poisonous to humans, but a healthy option for dwarves. An easy survival check will reveal the liquid is made from arsenic.
## History
[[Gunther Stone]] is the proprietor of this dish. His customer base may be small, but they are incredibly loyal. 

## Cultural Significance
A healthy dish for the small dwarf community within the walls of [[Nervios]].
