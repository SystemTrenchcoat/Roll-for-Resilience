## Description
When a character is having their thoughts read, they must write down at least three thoughts that their characters are having. Random chance and character stats determine which thought is read. (Examples: If a character is really intelligent, they might have a higher chance of having their masking thoughts revealed while less intelligent characters have greater chances of their active or secret thought being revealed)
Secret Thought: A deep secret the character is hiding. (Example: “I am a part of the rebellion”, “I don’t shower often”, etc).
Lowest chance of being read
Active Thoughts: Current goals, motivations, or plans the character is working towards (Examples: “I am going to rob that man”, “Gotta got to the market to buy fish”, “I want to beat the crap out of this guard”, etc)
Masking Thoughts: Thoughts that are meant to hide active or secret thoughts. Characters only decide this if they are actively trying to resist the mind reading. (Example: “Mmmmm eggs,”, “this guard smells awful”, “i hope the guard cant tell im wearing glass underwear right now”)
Can use any skill for the check if they can make a clever argument as to why they can.
